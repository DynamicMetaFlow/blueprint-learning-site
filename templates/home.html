Computer Science for All Blueprint

The Computer Science for All Blueprint is resource to help NYC public school educators bring meaningful computer science (CS) education to every student in their schools. You will find:

  • Student work that will get you excited about what your students can accomplish with computer science,
  • Educator-focused explanations of computer science practices and concepts as well as sample “I Can” statements for the K-2, 3-5, 6-8, and 9-12 grade bands,
  • Classroom-ready Exemplar Computer Science Units that illustrate a meaningful CS unit integrated with different subject areas,
  • Administrator and Teacher Resources, including videos of NYC CS classrooms and guides written by NYC public school teachers
